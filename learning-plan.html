    My Learning Plan

    What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

    Long term goal is to be a Web3 Developer, To particapate and help build all things Decentralized, And help change the world.
    The pathway to get there I believe is a long one, But a possible one.
    I will tend to start with front-end,work my way into Javascript, React, Typescript. From there I believe it will be more Backend, Then eventually Solidity and Smart Contracts.

    A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

    My own strengths I believe is having the self-drive and focus and consistency to keep learning and progressing on my pathway to who I want to become.
    My Limitations are Self belief which is a big one for me, That I have to get over and actually believe in myself.
    Another Limitation is working with others, and accomplishing things as a collective or a group. I have not had alot of experience working with others nor do i have the social tendencies to interact with alot people.
    This is something I recognize and something that I want to work on and overcome with the help from EDA and my peers.

    What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

    I would like to see my social tendencies and trust in others grow and develop.
    Also I would like to see my group project workload distribution and communication develop

    How I will manage my workload

    I will manage my workload by working to timestamps and taking it step by step, Without trying to look to far ahead
    To work safely with others, I belive communication is a big factor, We all have different responsibilities in our daily lives and understanding these is always a big help to everyone involved. Communicating these and coming to a mutual understanding of what is expected when from who will help everyone in the process.
    When Things build up, I take a break, decompress from all things screens/tech related and do something that is the complete opposite to what I was doing. This helps my brain to calm down and refresh.So when I go back to what I was doing, I can look at it with a new mindset.

    A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

    My first point of call is to always do my own research, and see if i can overcome my problem by myself. Failing this I would then ask my peers on "Slack". After that if I am still stuck I will contact a facilitator privately and work through the steps I have taken and what I am trying to achieve.

    A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

    I expect transperancy throughout the course, What is expected and how to go about it.
    Also clear communication when asked upon, and just overall support and kindness towards everyone
