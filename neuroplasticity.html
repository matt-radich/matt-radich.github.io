    Neuroplasticity

    Neuroplasticity

    understanding neuroplasticity benefits people by knowing that it is actaully the process by which all permanent learning takes place in your brain.
    This tends to make your brain resilient.
    This happens as neuroplasticity allows the nerve cells in your brain to adjust and over come things by forming new connections within your brain.

    Engaging with Neuroplasticity for your own benefit

    Start imagining how I can do certain things and go about certain things. As I can build the neuron strength within the brain before I do it physically.
    Make sure im coding/doing something program related each day, as repetition matters.
    If I keep doing this , I can improve, If I stop this I may lose the knowledge or ability I once had. Be resilient , As I know it is just my brain learning and improving.

    Some ways to increase your neuroplasticity

    Challenge your brain with activities that make it think, and solve problems.
    This may include games, Be it video games, puzzles, board games.
    Doing certain online games to do with coding are always good, To keep a certain way of thinking to solve problems. You can learn a new language to speak - this will improve attention, memory, creative thinking and reading comprehension. Some others include, Travel, Exercise, and be creative such as doing art.

    Growth Mindset

    Growth Mindset

    In a growth mindset, People believe that their abilities can be developed through different strategies, hard work and input from others,They believe their knowledge is not restricted.
    Growth mindset can help you overcome certain blocks or obstacles you may face when trying to learn something, or trying to develop a new skillset.
    With a growth mindset you understand the importance of persistence and determination. By changing the way we think and adapt, we can change the way we learn.

    Surprises or changes with this exploration

    Not much of a surprise as I have been researching and trying put growth mindset into practicet/still developing this way of thinking over the last 1 - 1/2 years. I have seen improvements in my learning and way of thinking while doing this.

    Integrating Growth Mindset into my learning journey

    Just to keep telling myself I dont know this "yet" but with perserverance and resilience I will.
    With knowing that our knowledge can grow without a ceiling is exciting and keeps me motivated when I hit a roadblock.
    This tells me, Im about to learn something new and grow my knowledge base, So keep going.
